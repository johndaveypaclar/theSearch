We’re going to talk about the two types of SEO, the On-page SEO and the Off-page SEO. The On-page SEO is what you do with your page/website/blog to enhance the traffic. It is what every web developer should consider. The Off-page SEO deals with many link building factors. It’s a nonstop or an ongoing process and needs regular new links that are given priority by each search engines. In short, if you can obtain a high-quality link, then your site can rank higher.

SEO guides for On-Page technique:

>Use SEO-friendly URLs. According to the most famous search engine, the first 3-5 words in a URL are given more weight. So, you should make your URL short. And don’t forget to include target keywords inside the URL.

>Start title with keyword. We all know that the title tag is the most important on-page SEO factor. So, the closer the keyword is to the beginning of the title tag, the more weight it has with search engines.

>Always add Modifiers to your title. Use “2017”, “guide”, and “review” can help you rank for long tail versions of your target keyword.

>Wrap your blog post title in an h1 tag. The headline tag is also known as the h1 tag.

>Dazzle with multimedia. Texts are boring, add some videos, images and even gifs.

>Wrap subheadings in h2 tag. Always include your target keyword in at least one subheading and wrap it in h2 tag.

>Drop keywords in first 100 words. The keyword should appear in the first 100-150 words in the blog/page.

>Use responsive design. The most famous search engine now penalizes mobile unfriendly sites last two years ago.

>Use outbound links. Outbound links helps the search engine to figure out your page’s topic.

>Use internal links. Internal linking is MONEY. Use it twice or thrice in every post.

>Boost sites speed. You can boost your site speed by using CDN, compressing your images, and switching to a faster host.

>Sprinkle LSI keywords. This will help the search engine determine the page’s relevancy.

>Image optimization. At least one image file name must include the target keyword.

>Use social sharing buttons. This may not play a direct role in ranking the site, but this can share your contents.

>Post Long Content. In SEO, “length is strength”. Longer contents tend to rank significantly higher than shorter contents.

>Boost Dwell time. Increase the average dwell time by writing long, engaging content that keeps people reading.

SEO guides for Off-page technique:

>Social media engagement. This is a major technique. If you want to make your content popular, engage with people on different social media platforms.

>Social bookmarking sites. One of the best platforms to promote the content. If you bookmark the content on popular social bookmarking websites, you gain high traffic to your webpage or blog.

>Forum submission. Always participate in search forums that are related to the website and business to make a community. You should reply to threads, and answer people’s questions.

>Article submission. Submit article in a high PR article directory. You can give your links to your website.

>Question and Answer. Give clear answers to question, give a link to your website which will increase the visibility.

>Video submission. If you want to have a popular video, give it a proper title, tags, description , and reference links.

>Infographic submission. You should make your infographics more creative. Always give reference link to your webpage or blog.

>Document sharing. Create good documents relating to the business or blog. These documents must have a unique content and should be in pdf or ppt format.
